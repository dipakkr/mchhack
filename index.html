About Us

We are orgainizing the first edition of Microsoft Campus Hackathon at Galgotias College of Engineering and Technology. MCH Hack 1.0 is platform for developers,designers and inventors to code, design and hack some amazing solution for real world problem. Whether we are first time or coders or a pro come join us, build something amzazing. We will provide you food, swags and fun to have an amazing weekend. We can not to see what you build at hackathon.

What we do ?

We at Microsoft Student Partners Community empowers students to code and introduce them with new technlogies by oraganzing meetups and workshops.

The Microsoft Student Partners is a worldwide recognizable program to sponsor undergraduate and postgraduate students majoring in disciplines related to technology, typically computer science, computer information systems, and information technology. MSP program aims to enhance students' employability and increase students' awareness of Microsoft technologies. Student Partners are given access to the latest Microsoft software, development tools, reference material, industry events, and training opportunities. MSPs further share the knowledge among the academic community by arranging courses; giving presentations and lectures; and initiating projects among the students.

How to Register :

  • Click on "Register now" button given above.

  • You will be redirected to HackSociety portal for registration.

  • Click on "EASY APPLY" button. You must have to complete your profile( Basic Info) before applying.

  • If applying individually, Choose "Individual", and you are done with registration. Goto "Dashboard" to see your applied hackathons.

  • If applying as Team, Choose "Team", and then Click on "Create Code", Now you have successfully registered a team. Go to "Dashboard" and you will see your see "Your Team Code". Tell other team members to enter that while registration.

  • Congrats!! You have succesfully registered for MCH Hack 1.0. Goto to "DashBoard" to check the status of your applied hackathon.

  • Once Succesfully applied, You will receive a mail with 24 - 48hrs for instruction regarding completing the further application process.

Note :
1. Completed application doesn't mean you are accepted for hackathon, We will release the first shortlist by 10th January,2018.

2.You have to pay the registration fee of Rs. 50 PER TEAM (Whether 1 or 4 member) after you get shortlisted confirmation mail.

Event FAQs

What is a hackathon ?

Hackathons are a place where many passionate professionals or students in Technology and Business Development actively participate - form diverse teams, make their pitches, adds their expertise and work towards creating a solution.

It is one such opportunity where you make many new friends from diverse backgrounds whom you would have never met, and learn new things from mentors & teammates. Above all, Hackathon helps you to see a problem from multiple perspectives and arrive at a solution.

Is there any registration fee ?

Yes, We are taking a nominal amount of Rs.50 per participants inorder to confirm your seat.

What are things I need to bring ?

You need to bring your own College IDs, Laptops, chargers,hardwares( for IOT hack ).

How big a team can be?

A team can have minimum 1 and maximum 4 members.

Who can participate?

The contest is open to students enrolled at university or in an engineering school during the academic year 2017-2018, from any grade.
