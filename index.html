About the Hackthon

This is the first edition of Microsoft Campus Hackathon at Galgotias College of Engineering and Technology. At MCH Hack 1.0, we’re inviting all the developers, designers and innovators from India to code, design and hack some amazing solutions for real world problems in 24 hours. Come along with your team and code the best sample to win amazing prizes, certificates and obviously recognition. We will provide you food, swags and fun to have an amazing weekend with like-minded people. Get ready for the event and register asap because here, time is the only limit.

About Us

We at Microsoft Student Partners Community empower students to code, and introduce them new technologies by organizing technical meetups and workshops.

The ‘Microsoft Student Partners’ is a worldwide recognizable program to sponsor undergraduate and postgraduate students majoring in disciplines related to technology, typically computer science, computer information systems, and information technology. MSP program aims to enhance students' employability and increase students' awareness of Microsoft technologies. Student Partners are given access to the latest Microsoft software, development tools, reference materials, industry events, and training opportunities. MSPs further share the knowledge among the academic community by conducting workshops, hackathons, tech-fests, and initiating projects among the students.

How to Register

  • Click on "Register now" button given above.

  • You will be redirected to HackSociety portal for registration.

  • Click on "EASY APPLY" button. You must have to complete your profile( Basic Info) before applying.

  • If applying individually, Choose "Individual", and you are done with registration. Goto "Dashboard" to see your applied hackathons.

  • If applying as a Team, Choose "Team", and then Click on "Create Code", Now you have successfully registered a team. Go to "Dashboard" and you will see "Your Team Code". Tell other team members to enter that while registration.

  • Congrats!! You have successfully registered for MCH Hack 1.0. Goto to "DashBoard" to check the status of your applied hackathon.

  • Once Succesfully applied, You will receive a mail within 24 - 48hrs for instructions regarding completion of the further application process.

Note :
1. Completed application doesn't mean you are accepted for hackathon, We will release the first shortlist by 16th January, 2018.

2. You have to pay the registration fee of Rs. 50 PER TEAM (Whether 1 or 4 members) after you get shortlisted confirmation mail.

For any other query or trouble during registration, Mail us : admin@mchgalgotias.tech

Event FAQs

What is a hackathon ?

Hackathons are a place where many passionate professionals or students in Technology and Business Development actively participate - form diverse teams, make their pitches, adds their expertise and work towards creating a solution.

It is one such opportunity where you make many new friends from diverse backgrounds whom you would have never met, and learn new things from mentors & teammates. Above all, Hackathon helps you to see a problem from multiple perspectives and arrive at a solution.

Is there any registration fee ?

Yes, We are taking a nominal amount of Rs.50 per participants inorder to confirm your seat.

What are things I need to bring ?

You need to bring your own College IDs, Laptops, chargers,hardwares( for IOT hack ).

How big a team can be?

A team can have minimum 1 and maximum 4 members.

Who can participate?

The contest is open to students enrolled at university or in an engineering school during the academic year 2017-2018, from any grade.
